About us

Be more in sync with
your team in Obiri

No more silos or back-and-forth emails. With OBIRI, your entire team works from the same source of truth — finance, HR, operations, and sales, all connected. Whether you're in a hotel, restaurant, school, or service business, collaboration becomes seamless, fast, and crystal clear.

Why teams love it

  • Shared Dashboards – Everyone sees the same numbers, the same progress, the same updates. Transparency builds trust.
  • Real-Time Updates – Changes made in Lagos show instantly in Abuja. No delays, no missing data.
  • Clear Accountability – Assign tasks, track owners, and never lose sight of responsibilities again.
