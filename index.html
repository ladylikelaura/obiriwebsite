Manage Every Location
From One Central Dashboard

Whether you run multiple branches, warehouses, outlets, or an entire group of businesses, OBIRI keeps everything connected. Each location operates independently — but you still get full oversight of sales, stock, expenses, staff activity, and performance in real time.

Why multi-location businesses rely on OBIRI

  • Centralized Control – Monitor all branches from a single dashboard without disrupting how each team works locally.
  • Multi-Tenant Separation – Each location or business unit gets its own clean environment, data, and permissions — but you can switch between them instantly.
  • Unified Reporting – Compare performance across locations, track trends, spot low stock, and make decisions backed by real numbers.
